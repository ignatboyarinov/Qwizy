Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Qwizy is a trivia app where you can play solo, with friends, or against random opponents. Each match contains 6 questions, and your performance is recorded for points.

2. Data Collection

When you sign up for Qwizy, you can use Google, Facebook, Apple login, or email. We collect information from these platforms as applicable, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Profile information (which can be edited)
  • Your nickname (visible to other players)

Qwizy also collects the following data:

  • Game Data: Your match history, scores, and in-game statistics
  • Ad Data: We use AdMob for advertisements, which may collect data for a better ad experience
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics is used to improve the app experience

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used to:

  • Facilitate your gaming experience by recording matches
  • Improve the app through analytics
  • Provide relevant advertisements via AdMob

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with third-party providers, including:

  • AdMob: For relevant advertisements
  • Firebase: For analytics and app performance

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Delete your account and request that all related data, including match recordings, be deleted
  • Opt out of certain data collection where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 8.

6. Data Security

We follow stringent security measures to protect your data, leveraging the Firebase platform on Google. This includes:

  • Encryption of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits
  • Restricted access to personal information

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If changes occur, we will notify you via appropriate channels.
